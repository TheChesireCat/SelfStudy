# TOTAL NUMBER OF LETTERS

(There are in all two hundred and Forty Seven Letters in Tamil excluding Grantha letters. They are vowels 12, Consonants 18 ; 'Uyirmey Azhuthu' <PAIR lang1="ta" lang2="en">(உயிர்மெய் எழுத்து)</PAIR> 216 and one Aaytha Azhuthu <PAIR lang1="ta" lang2="en">(ஆய்த எழுத்து)</PAIR>.)

## Formation

The Uyirmey Azhuthu is formed by combining Vowel and Consonant :

### Example :

*   க் + அ = க
    K + A = KA
*   க் + ஆ = கா
    K + AA = KAA
*   க் + இ = கி
    K + E = KE
*   க் + ஈ = கீ
    K + EE = KEE
*   க் + உ = கு
    K + U = KU
