### CLASSIFICATION OF VOWELS

(Vowels are classified into two groups as *short* (Kuril) and as *long* (Nedil). The words short and long do not refer to the form or figure of the letters. They refer to the sound of the pronunciation.

#### Short Vowels

| Tamil | English |
|---|---|
| அ | A |
| இ | E |
| உ | U |
| எ | $\bar{A}$ |
| ஒ | O |

#### Long Vowels

| Tamil | English |
|---|---|
| ஆ | AA |
| ஈ | EE |
| ஊ | OO |
| ஏ | AE |
| ஓ | OH |

The letters 'ஐ' (I) and 'ஔ' (OU) common in pronunciation but are called Long letters in grammar.
