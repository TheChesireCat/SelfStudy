Vowels
</PAIR>

In English there are only five vowels. But in Tamil there are twelve vowels. While pronouncing English letters for Tamil letters and words, please read as they are and not as they are pronounced in a word.

| Tamil | English |
|---|---|
| அ | A (ah) |
| ஆ | AA (aah) |
| இ | E |
| ஈ | EE |
| உ | U |
| ஊ | OO |
| எ | $\bar{A}$ |
| ஏ | AE |
| ஐ | I |
| ஒ | O |
| ஓ | OH |
| ஔ | OU |
| ஃ | AKH |

1. Pronounce the letter A as the first A in the word 'AMERICA'
2. For the letters AA pronounce as you do in the word 'FATHER.'
3. For the 3rd vowel the letter E must be pronounced as I is pronounced in the word 'INDIAN.'
4. For the 4th vowel the letters EE should be pronounced as in the word 'SWEET.'
5. For the 5th vowel pronounce the letter without 'IY' sound as in the word 'PUT.'
6. For the 6th vowel pronounce the two Os as you do in the word 'TOOL.'
7. For the letter AE pronounce as the word 'ELEPHANT.'
8. The 8th vowel AE should be pronounced as A is pronounced in the word 'ATE'.
9. The 9th vowel 'I' should be pronounced as in the word 'ISLAND.'
10. The 10th vowel O should be pronounced as the first O in the word 'OPINION.'
11. OH should be pronounced as in the word 'OVER.'
