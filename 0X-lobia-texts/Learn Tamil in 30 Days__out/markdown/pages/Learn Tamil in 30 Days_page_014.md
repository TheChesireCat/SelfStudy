### Just a Minute...

Kindly read the instructions given below carefully before you go through the book.

(i) Tamil letters are written from left to right in clockwise direction. There is a chart on the next page.

(ii) When there are 'ee' letters, pronounce it as you do in the word 'meet'. When you come across the letters 'ae' together pronounce it as in the word 'aerial'. For the letter 'O' pronounce it as in the word 'only'. 'OH' is to be pronounced as in the word 'over'.

(iii) If you do not have time to go through the entire book, you can learn by heart some of the sentences relating to matters which are of interest to you.

(iv) Your suggestions for improving the book and making it a more useful one to beginners, are welcome.
