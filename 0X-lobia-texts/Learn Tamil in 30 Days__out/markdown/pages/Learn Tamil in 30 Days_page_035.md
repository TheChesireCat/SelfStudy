# SPECIAL LETTERS

[There are six special letters adopted from Sanskrit called GRANTHA LETTERS]

- <PAIR lang1="ta" lang2="en">ஜ --||-- JA</PAIR>
- <PAIR lang1="ta" lang2="en">ஸ --||-- SSA</PAIR>
- <PAIR lang1="ta" lang2="en">ஷ --||-- SHA</PAIR>
- <PAIR lang1="ta" lang2="en">க்ஷ --||-- TCHA, KSHA</PAIR>
- <PAIR lang1="ta" lang2="en">ஹ --||-- HA</PAIR>
- <PAIR lang1="ta" lang2="en">ஸ்ரீ --||-- SHRI</PAIR>

1. For the Second letter emphasis is on two 'S's.
2. For the 3rd letter pronounce the SH with the tip of the tongue upward in the mouth not touching any where.
